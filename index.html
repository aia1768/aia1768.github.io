What we do

We make first person shooter games that provide gamers everywhere to have a feel of technology as well as adding some survival learning here and there...

What is different about our games?

Unlike TF2, overwatch and all those other f.p.s games, our games include many features such as gliding, destruction of buildings, vehicles and much much more...

Where can you buy our games?

After the release of each game, we'll try our best to release it globally so that everyone can get access to them.

How do we get money?

Our games themeselves will be free like Pokemon Go but we'll get our money from in app purchases to boost your experience.

What devices can you play our games on?

Mainly, our games will be accessible on mac, windows and other sorts of computers.

What's the battery usage of our games?

We will try our very best to make the games battery efficient: 1% will be used around every 5-10 minutes.
